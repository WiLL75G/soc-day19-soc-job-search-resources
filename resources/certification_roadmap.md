# SOC Analyst Certification Roadmap

## Overview

Certifications signal credibility to hiring managers.
For an entry-level SOC analyst the right certifications
unlock doors that experience alone cannot open yet.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Do These First)

### CompTIA Security+
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | ~$392 USD |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner — Medium |
| **Time to Prepare** | 2–3 months |
| **Why Get It** | Industry standard — required by many employers and DoD |
| **Study Resource** | Professor Messer (free), CompTIA CertMaster |
| **Exam Code** | SY0-701 |

---

### CompTIA Network+
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | ~$338 USD |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Time to Prepare** | 1–2 months |
| **Why Get It** | Proves networking fundamentals — critical for SOC work |
| **Study Resource** | Professor Messer (free), Mike Meyers Udemy course |
| **Exam Code** | N10-009 |

---

## Phase 2 — SOC Specific (Get These Next)

### CompTIA CySA+
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | ~$392 USD |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Time to Prepare** | 2–3 months |

| **Exam Code** | CS0-003 |

## Phase 3 — Advanced (After Landing First Role)

### Splunk Core Certified User
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | Free (exam ~$130) |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner — Intermediate |
| **Time to Prepare** | 1 month |
| **Why Get It** | Splunk is the #1 SIEM — proves tool proficiency |
| **URL** | education.splunk.com |

---

### Microsoft SC-200 — Security Operations Analyst
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | ~$165 USD |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Time to Prepare** | 2–3 months |
| **Why Get It** | Proves Microsoft Sentinel and Defender proficiency |

---

### SANS GIAC GSEC
| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | ~$7,000 USD (employer sponsored) |
| **Difficulty** | Advanced |
| **Time to Prepare** | 3–6 months |
